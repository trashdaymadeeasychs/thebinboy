Common Questions

Trash Bin Cleaning FAQ

Everything you need to know about our bin cleaning service in Charleston, SC.

How often should I clean my trash bins?

For most households in Charleston, a monthly clean is ideal — especially in South Carolina's heat and humidity, which speeds up bacteria growth and odour. Quarterly works well if your bins don't get heavy organic waste. Our Monthly Plan at $20/month is our most popular for exactly this reason.

Is your bin cleaning service safe for kids and pets?

Yes. We use biodegradable, non-toxic odour-neutralizing solution that's completely safe for children, pets, and your garden. Everything we use is eco-friendly and washes away cleanly.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Nope. Just leave your bins at the curb or driveway and we'll handle everything. We'll text you when we're on the way and again when we're done — and we'll return your bins to your garage or wherever you prefer.

What areas do you service near Charleston?

We currently service Charleston, Goose Creek, Mount Pleasant, Moncks Corner, Johns Island, Hanahan, Folly Beach, and James Island. Monthly routes are based on customer density in each area — if your neighborhood isn't covered yet, get in touch and we'll add you to the waitlist.

How does payment and scheduling work?

Fill out the booking form below or give Bryan a call at (843) 478-0108. We'll confirm your service date and send a reminder before each visit. Monthly customers are billed monthly — no long-term contracts, cancel any time.
